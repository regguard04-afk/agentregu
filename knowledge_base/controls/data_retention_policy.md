# Data Retention Policy

**Control ID:** DR-001  
**Version:** 1.4  
**Last Updated:** 2025-11-20  
**Owner:** Legal & Compliance Team  
**Classification:** Internal  

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the requirements for retaining, archiving, and disposing of organizational data in compliance with legal, regulatory, and business requirements.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all data created, received, maintained, or transmitted by the organization across all formats (electronic, physical, cloud-based).

## 3. Retention Periods

### 3.1 Financial Records
- General ledger: 7 years
- Tax records: 7 years
- Audit reports: 7 years
- Invoices and receipts: 5 years

### 3.2 Employee Records
- Employment records: 7 years after termination
- Payroll records: 7 years
- Training records: Duration of employment + 3 years
- Background check results: 2 years

### 3.3 Customer Data
- Active customer records: Duration of relationship + 5 years
- KYC/AML records: 5 years after account closure (per regulatory requirements)
- Transaction records: 7 years
- Customer communications: 3 years

### 3.4 Regulatory and Compliance Data
- Compliance reports: 7 years
- Regulatory filings: Permanent
- Audit trails: 7 years
- Incident reports: 5 years after resolution

### 3.5 IT and Security Data
- System logs: 1 year minimum, 3 years recommended
- Security event logs: 3 years
- Email archives: 3 years
- Backup tapes: Follow source data retention + 1 year

## 4. Data Classification and Handling

| Classification | Description | Retention Default |
|---|---|---|
| Public | Non-sensitive, publicly available | 1 year |
| Internal | Internal business use | 3 years |
| Confidential | Restricted access | 5 years |
| Regulated | Subject to regulatory requirements | Per regulation |

## 5. Data Disposal

- Electronic data: Cryptographic erasure or NIST 800-88 compliant destruction
- Physical documents: Cross-cut shredding (DIN 66399 Level P-4 or higher)
- Storage media: Degaussing followed by physical destruction
- Certificate of destruction required for Confidential and Regulated data

## 6. Legal Hold

- All retention schedules are suspended upon legal hold notice
- Legal holds managed by General Counsel
- Custodians notified within 24 hours of hold initiation
- Quarterly hold reminders issued to all custodians

## 7. Compliance References
- GDPR: Article 5(1)(e) — Storage limitation
- SOX: Section 802 — Document retention
- HIPAA: 45 CFR §164.530(j) — 6-year retention
- RBI Master Direction: KYC record retention requirements
- SEC Rule 17a-4: Broker-dealer record retention
