# Employee Security Policy

**Control ID:** ES-001  
**Version:** 2.2  
**Last Updated:** 2025-11-25  
**Owner:** HR & Security Team  
**Classification:** Internal  

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines security responsibilities, training requirements, and behavioral expectations for all employees to protect organizational assets and maintain compliance with regulatory requirements.

## 2. Scope

All full-time employees, part-time employees, interns, temporary staff, and contractors working on behalf of the organization.

## 3. Pre-Employment Security

### 3.1 Background Checks
- Criminal background check for all employees before start date.
- Credit check for roles with financial access or fiduciary responsibility.
- Employment and education verification.
- Reference checks (minimum 2 professional references).
- Enhanced screening for roles with access to sensitive/regulated data.

### 3.2 Employment Agreements
- Confidentiality and non-disclosure agreement (NDA) signed before day one.
- Acceptable use policy acknowledgment.
- Security policy acknowledgment.
- Intellectual property assignment agreement.

## 4. Security Awareness Training

### 4.1 Required Training
| Training | Frequency | Audience |
|---|---|---|
| Security Awareness Fundamentals | Annual + at onboarding | All employees |
| Phishing Awareness | Quarterly simulation + training | All employees |
| Data Privacy & GDPR | Annual | All employees handling personal data |
| Secure Development Practices | Annual | Engineering and development teams |
| Incident Response Procedures | Annual | IT and security staff |
| Insider Threat Awareness | Annual | All employees |
| Regulatory Compliance | Annual | Compliance and risk teams |

### 4.2 Training Completion
- Training must be completed within 30 days of assignment.
- Non-completion escalated to department manager at 14 days.
- Access restrictions applied for overdue mandatory training (after 30 days).
- Training completion tracked in the LMS with certificates.

## 5. Acceptable Use

### 5.1 Corporate Devices
- Corporate devices for business use; limited personal use permitted.
- No installation of unauthorized software.
- Encryption required on all corporate devices.
- Screen lock after 5 minutes of inactivity.
- Report lost or stolen devices within 1 hour.

### 5.2 Email and Communications
- No transmission of classified data via unencrypted email.
- Caution with external email recipients (external tag warnings enabled).
- No auto-forwarding of corporate email to personal accounts.
- Encrypted messaging for sensitive internal communications.

### 5.3 Internet Use
- Web filtering applied to block known malicious and inappropriate categories.
- Cloud storage for business data only via approved services.
- No use of personal VPN services on corporate networks.

## 6. Clean Desk and Screen Policy
- Sensitive documents locked away when not in use.
- Clear desk at end of each business day.
- Screen lock when leaving workstation unattended.
- Whiteboards erased after meetings involving sensitive topics.
- Printers: retrieve printed documents immediately.

## 7. Termination and Offboarding
- Access revocation within 24 hours of termination notification.
- Return of all corporate assets (laptop, badges, tokens) on last day.
- Exit interview including security reminder obligations.
- Ongoing NDA obligations communicated in writing.
- Reassignment of shared resource access and ownership.

## 8. Reporting Obligations
- All employees must report suspected security incidents immediately.
- Whistle-blower protection for good-faith security reports.
- Report security policy violations to security@company.com.
- No retaliation for reporting suspected violations.

## 9. Compliance References
- ISO 27001: A.7.1, A.7.2, A.7.3 (Human resource security)
- NIST 800-53: AT-1, AT-2, AT-3, AT-4 (Awareness and training)
- SOC 2: CC1.4 (Board and management oversight)
- GDPR: Article 39 (Tasks of the DPO — awareness raising)
- PCI DSS: Requirement 12.6 (Security awareness program)
